Welcome to Pooja Petals Boutique

Discover the finest selection of traditional Hindu flower garlands at Pooja Petals Boutique. As a small, heartfelt business owned by a passionate young woman working toward her college degree, every garland is created with dedication, patience, and love. Each piece is handcrafted from fresh blooms, thoughtfully arranged to honor your spiritual rituals, celebrations, and cherished traditions. At Pooja Petals Boutique, you're not just purchasing a garland, you're supporting a dream, a journey, and an artisan who pours her soul into every petal.
